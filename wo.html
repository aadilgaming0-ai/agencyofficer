Assumptions Considered

  • WRP and WCP are auto-calculated based on policy count × ATS × applicable product credit.
  • Calculator uses maximum product credit as per the defined slabs for all product categories.
  • ATS (annual premium in lakhs) determines the product slab for credit% and FYC%.
  • Self Commission = Σ(WRP × FYC%) from all self-sourced policies.
  • L1 Commission is included only for MPI calculation, not added into AO Incentive directly.
  • MPI (%) is applied on total commission (Self + L1) based on the WCP incentive table.
  • L1 Activation Bonus is based solely on number of Active L1, not their premium size.
  • A fixed 0.02L payout is added to AO Incentive for every AO.
  • AO Incentive = Self Commission + MPI + L1 Activation Bonus + 0.02L.
  • Activation Kicker and Cash Rewards depend only on WCP slabs.
  • Trip eligibility has no monetary value, only an informational label.
  • % of WRP = (AO Incentive + AO Reward) ÷ WRP × 100.
  • All amounts are assumed to be in Lakhs (L).
  • Premium values are assumed to be annualised and paid (FYP).
  • All ATS inputs are assumed to fall within valid product slabs.
  • Each L1 is assumed to produce uniform NOP and ATS as entered.
  • No deductions like GST, TDS, or clawbacks are applied.
  • Product credit does not exceed the maximum credit % defined in the slabs.
